AURA Privacy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

AURA is a social entertainment app. Scan results are designed to work without long-term result storage.

If you join the launch list, we collect the email address you submit so we can send launch updates. Netlify may process that form submission on our behalf.

If AI analysis is enabled, prompts may be sent to a server endpoint for processing. API keys must stay on the server and are never intended to be embedded in the iOS app.

AURA results are for entertainment only and are not medical, psychological, educational, employment, or financial advice.
